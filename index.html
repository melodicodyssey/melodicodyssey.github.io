About Me



Hello there!

My name is Stephen Bauer and I love to write code. Those moments when a piece of beautiful code just works perfectly for the first time, are the moments with which I want to saturate my programming career.

I had always had a interest in programming/coding but for various absurd reasons never followed through with it. After receiving a Bachelor's Degree in Accounting and being unhappy with the career path I felt no choice but to head down, I finally decided to perform a major course-correction after being let go from my last position in 2013.
